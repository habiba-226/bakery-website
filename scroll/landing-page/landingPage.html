history

Founded in 2020, Petite Port began as a small, home-based bakery driven by a passion for creating bite-sized delights. What started as a hobby soon flourished into a beloved local business, known for its perfect blend of quality, creativity, and charm. Specializing in mini portions of classic bakery items, Petite Port offers everything from cupcakes to croissants, each crafted with the finest ingredients and an artisanâ€™s touch.

vision

Petite Port adds a twist to your everyday bakeries so people can indulge in all their favorite baked goodies and try different combinations without breaking the bank. The name is simple and self-explanatory for easy understanding. Petite means mini or little in French and Port stands for portions. It represents elegance without the hassle of trying to afford it, making it the perfect bakery for everyone.

goals

At Petite Port, our mission is to bring joy to everyday moments through our delicately crafted, small-sized treats. We aim to create a cozy and welcoming environment where every customer feels at home. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community drives us to innovate and continuously improve, ensuring that each visit to Petite Port is a delightful experience.
